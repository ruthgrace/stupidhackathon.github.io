THE SAN FRANCISCO STUPID SHIT NO ONE NEEDS AND TERRIBLE IDEAS "HACKATHON"

Have you ever been at a hackathon and seen a profoundly useless and/or terrible project? This is like that, but for every project. The SFSSNNATI hackathon will be held at Noisebridge @ 2169 Mission St, 3rd Floor on Saturday, October 19th at 10 AM. Come out alone or with friends, with ideas or without - just be ready to hack with new friends and old on the most stupid shit you can think of. The event will start with team formation and brainstorming, so come out even if you can't think of anything quite useless enough to meet your lowest standards. Hardware, software, something in between - we're open to just about anything! Projects will demo at 1 PM on Sunday, October 20th!
